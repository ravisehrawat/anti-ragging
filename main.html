It's Not an "Introduction," It's Abuse.

Ragging is any act that causes or is likely to cause physical or psychological harm, embarrassment, or annoyance to a new student.

  • Forcing students to perform humiliating or ridiculous acts.
  • Verbal abuse, insults, or offensive language.
  • Physical assault or threatening behavior.
  • Exploiting students for financial or personal gain.

Key takeaway: Ragging is a violation of human rights.

Ragging Comes in Many Forms‚ÄîAll are Harmful.

Verbal & Written

Psychological

Creating fear, causing emotional distress, social ostracism, and cyberbullying.

Physical

Pushing, slapping, forcing strenuous physical activity, or any form of assault.

Sexual

Inappropriate touching, sexually explicit comments, or forcing obscene gestures.

The Scars Last a Lifetime.

Psychological Trauma

  • Anxiety, depression, and withdrawal
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Loss of self-esteem and confidence

Academic Consequences

  • Poor concentration and declining grades
  • Increased absenteeism & drop-out rates

Physical Harm

  • Injuries ranging from minor to severe
  • In extreme cases, it can lead to death or suicide

The Law is Clear: Ragging is a CRIME.

Ignorance of the Law is No Excuse.

Legal Framework (India):

UGC Regulations (2009): Makes ragging a punishable offense.

Indian Penal Code (IPC): Sections 323, 506 and others apply.

Severe Punishments Include:

  • Suspension or expulsion from the institution.
  • Imprisonment and heavy fines.
  • Debarment from admission to any other institution.
  • A permanent negative mark on your academic record.
